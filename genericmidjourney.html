United Arab Emirates pavilion- Expo 2025

Project Duration: Sep 2023 â€“ Sep 2023

In this project, we aim to design a pavilion for Expo 2025 using Midjourney artificial intelligence. The design draws inspiration from the previous years' pavilions of the United Arab Emirates and incorporates concepts derived from the rich culture and lifestyle of its people.

Our approach leverages advanced AI technology to create a pavilion that not only reflects the architectural brilliance of past UAE pavilions but also embodies the essence of Emirati traditions and modernity. By integrating cultural elements such as traditional materials, motifs, and sustainable practices, we strive to create a space that is both innovative and deeply rooted in heritage.
